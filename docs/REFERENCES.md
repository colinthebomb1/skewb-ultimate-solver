## Notes From References

- The Skewb Ultimate is a dodecahedron-shaped Skewb-family puzzle.
- Modern mass-produced versions use 12 colors.
- The mechanism is equivalent to the original Skewb.
- Unlike a normal Skewb, center orientation is visible on the Skewb Ultimate.
- Grubiks lists the state space as 100,776,960 positions and states every position can be solved in 14 moves or fewer.
